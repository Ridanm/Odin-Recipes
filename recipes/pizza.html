Recipe for Pizza

Learn how to prepare this Uruguayan Pizza.

This recipe makes about 4 servings.

Ingredients for the dough

  • Water 300 cc
  • Flour 0000 500 grams
  • Yeats 20 grams
  • Olive oil 1 tablespoon
  • salt 1 small tablespoon

Topping

  • Tomato sauce 300 grams
  • Green olives
  • Mozzarella 300 grams

Preparation of the dough

  1. Make a crown with the flour and salt.
  2. In the center add yeast, olive oil, water and mix little by little until the dough forms.
  3. Knead until you obtain 2 long buns and let rise on a plate with flour and plastic wrap on top.
  4. Stretch with your hands, leaving the edge wider and giving it an elongated shape.
  5. Place tomato sauce, grated cheese, and grated mozzarella on the dough, except for the edges
  6. Place in a wood-fired oven or kitchen oven at 250°C until the dough is cooked and the mozarella melts.
  7. The time in the oven 20 to 30 minutes
  8. Add sliced ​​green olives, optional (oregano, pesto) and cut into strips.
