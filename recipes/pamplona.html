Recipe for Pamplona

Learn how to prepare this recipe from Pamplona

Preparation time about 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • Whole uncut meat 1 kilo
  • Roasted red bell pepper 1
  • Mozzarella 200 grams
  • Smoked bacon 150 grams
  • Provoleta cheese 150 grams
  • Green olives 100 grams
  • Salt
  • Potatoes 500 grams

Preparation

  1. Open the meat lengthwise like a book until you obtain a thin, rectangular piece.
  2. Prepare the meat with all the seasonings and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Arrange on the bottom of the meat, leaving 2 cm from the edge, sticks of Provoleta cheese,
  4. strips of roasted bell pepper, smoked bacon lardons and 2 large sticks of mozarella, green olives.
  5. Roll out, leaving the filling very firm.
  6. Cut in half to obtain 2 rolls.
  7. Wrap each one in aluminum foil and grill over moderate heat for 20 minutes.
  8. Turn and cook for 20 more minutes.
  9. Cut the papines in halves and place on a hot grill with olive oil, salt, rosemary and saut√© until golden.
